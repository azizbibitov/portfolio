About Me

👋 I've spent the last 5+ years building iOS apps that ship and stay in the App Store. Video streaming, logistics, e-commerce, social, delivery. I've worked on all of them, usually owning the iOS side from the first screen to the release build.

Here's what I've shipped:

  • 💬 Salam Messenger - Social app with real-time chat, posts, stories, voice messages and push notifications.
  • 📰 SalamNews - Multilingual news app with categories, bookmarks and localization.

  • 🛒 Meninki - Marketplace MVP with Stripe payments and Firebase backend. Live with real users.
  • 💕 MIU LOVE - Online store with product catalog, cart and checkout flow.
  • 🤸 KinesteX - AI fitness app. Joined mid-development, redesigned the UI and added new features to a live codebase.
  • 📱 Cl:k - Social content-sharing app. Redesigned the story feed, added GIF and voice note chat, link preview cards and Share Extension.
  • ⬇️ YT Downloader - Video downloader with background downloads and media library.
  • 💬 iMessenger - Messaging app with real-time chat and media sharing.
  • 🔄 Content Transfer - Smart Share app for transferring content between devices.
  • 🎵 Live Concert - Live music streaming app with event listings and ticketing.

If you need a full MVP, I can take it the whole way: the app, a Firebase backend, payments, and getting it through App Store review. I work mostly in Swift with SwiftUI and UIKit, and I'm comfortable developing for multiple Apple platforms, including iPadOS, macOS, and watchOS.

I write code that the next person can actually read and extend, I keep you updated throughout the process, and I don't vanish once the project is delivered.
