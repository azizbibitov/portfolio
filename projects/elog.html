Features

What it does

A fully compliant ELD solution helping commercial drivers log hours of service, manage vehicle inspections, and stay connected with dispatchers in real time.

📊

HOS Log Management

Automatically tracks Hours of Service with duty status changes (Driving, On Duty, Off Duty, Sleeper Berth, Yard Move, Personal Conveyance). Logs are stored locally in GRDB and synced to Salesforce.

🔵

Bluetooth ELD Connection

Connects to IOSiX ELD hardware devices via Bluetooth. Receives engine-on/off, motion start/stop, periodic and cached records directly from the truck's ECM for automatic log generation.

📋

DVIR Inspections

Driver Vehicle Inspection Reports with pre- and post-trip vehicle condition checks. Drivers select unit and trailer defects, add signatures, and submit DVIRs for mechanic review.

📈

Real-Time Dashboard

Interactive timeline chart showing today's duty status over 24 hours. Displays drive, break, shift, and cycle time countdowns with visual HOS violation and warning indicators.

💬

Dispatch Chat

In-app messaging between driver and dispatcher with text, image, file, and voice message support. Unread badge count on the tab bar keeps drivers informed of new messages.

📍

Location Tracking

Continuous background GPS tracking sends driver location to the Salesforce backend. Location data is cached locally when offline and synced on reconnection.

Technology

Tech Stack

Built in SwiftUI with MVVM and Resolver for dependency injection. GRDB handles local persistence, AFNetworking drives REST calls to a Salesforce backend, and IOSiX SDK connects to Bluetooth ELD hardware.
