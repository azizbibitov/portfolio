Features

What it does

A Netflix-style streaming app serving Turkmenistan - full catalog browsing, live channels, offline playback, subscription billing, and a native video player with ad integration.

🎬

Video Catalog

Browse films, series, and collections organized by genre, category, and trending. Detailed content pages show cast, synopsis, related titles, and episode lists for multi-season shows.

📺

Live TV Channels

Watch live broadcast channels with real-time streaming. Channels are grouped by category with thumbnail previews, and playback switches seamlessly between on-demand and live mode.

▶️

Video Player

Custom AVKit-powered player with portrait and landscape modes, picture-in-picture, playback speed control, subtitle selection, and VAST pre-roll / mid-roll ad parsing and playback.

⬇️

Offline Downloads

Download films and episodes for offline viewing via AVAssetDownloadTask backed by CoreData. Downloads queue with progress tracking, Wi-Fi-only option, and persistent local library.

💳

Subscriptions

Tiered subscription plans with in-app purchase and server-side validation. Subscribers unlock ad-free playback, HD quality, and download access across all content.

🔍

Search and Explore

Full-text search across the catalog with real-time suggestions. Explore tab surfaces curated collections, new releases, and personalized recommendations based on watch history.

Technology

Tech Stack

Built in SwiftUI with MVVM and Factory for dependency injection. AVKit drives video and download, CoreData persists offline content, and a shared codebase targets both iOS and tvOS.
