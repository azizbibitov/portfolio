Features

What it does

A SwiftUI sound effects app — browse and mix concert ambience sounds, layer multiple tracks, and use with any live performance.

🎵

Sound Library

A rich categorised library of concert and event sounds — crowd noise, applause, instrument ambience, and more.

🎛️

Sound Mixer

Layer up to 4 sounds simultaneously with individual volume controls to create a custom live atmosphere mix.

🔍

Genre Browser

Browse sounds by genre with a grid layout and category tabs. Tap any sound to preview before adding to your mix.

🌟

Explore Tab

Curated explore feed with featured sound packs and new arrivals, backed by a full-screen parallax header image.

💎

Premium / IAP

Freemium model with in-app purchases via StoreKit to unlock the full sound library and premium categories.

💾

Saved Mixes

Persist custom mixes locally with Realm so users can reload their favourite combination instantly.
